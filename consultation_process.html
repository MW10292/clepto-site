Common questions about our process

We believe in transparency from the very first conversation

What if you can't help us?

We'll tell you honestly. Not every business needs AI, and not every AI project makes sense. If we're not the right fit, we'll explain why and point you toward better alternatives. Our reputation is built on honesty, not closing every deal.

How do you ensure our data stays secure?

Data security is non-negotiable. We sign NDAs before any detailed discussions, use encrypted communication channels, and follow enterprise-grade security protocols. Your data never leaves your systems during the consultation phase, and we're happy to work within your existing security frameworks.

What makes your approach different?

Most AI companies start with their technology and try to fit your business around it. We start with your business and build technology around that. We're not trying to sell you a pre-built solution - we're trying to understand your unique challenges and create something that actually solves them.

How much does the consultation cost?

The initial discovery call is completely free. If we move forward with a business analysis, we'll discuss pricing transparently based on the scope of work. We believe in earning your trust through value, not through high-pressure sales tactics.

What if we're not ready to implement AI yet?

That's perfectly fine. Sometimes the best advice is to wait. We'll help you understand when the timing might be better and what to focus on in the meantime. Building a relationship is more important to us than making a quick sale.
